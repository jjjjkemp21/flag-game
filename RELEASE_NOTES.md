## Globe Mode & geography mastery

*A new way to study — spin the planet, find the country.*

**What's new**

- **Globe Mode** — a 3D world you can spin, zoom, and tap. Pick the country whose flag you're shown, then double-tap (or hit Confirm) to lock it in.
- **Find-on-globe focus** — tap any country and the globe smoothly rotates and semi-zooms onto it, so your guess is unmistakable before you commit.
- **Hint** — stuck? One tap rotates the globe toward the correct continent. Half XP if you still get it right, but your streak stays intact.

**Geography mastery — a whole new axis**

- Globe mode tracks a brand-new "placed on the globe" stat per country, separate from your flag-recognition mastery. Same flags, two skills.
- Your Stats screen now has a Geography Mastery ring next to the existing one.

**New leaderboard scope**

- A **Globe** tab on the leaderboard ranks players by countries placed on the globe — both Global and Friends filters apply.

**New titles & achievements**

- A parallel ladder of geography titles: Pin Dropper → Roamer → Globe Wanderer → Pathfinder → Trailblazer → Topographer → Geomancer → Continental Master → **Atlas Cartographer**. Pick one to wear next to your name.
- 14 new achievements in a fresh "Globe" group, including per-continent Atlases and the legendary Atlas Cartographer for placing every country.
