## Accounts, Leaderboards & Friends

*The biggest Flag Quest update yet — your progress now travels with you.*

**What's new**

- **Accounts** — create a profile to back up your progress. Guest play still works with no sign-up required.
- **Global leaderboard** — climb the ranks with a single **XP** score earned from correct answers, mastered flags, and bonus-mode high scores.
- **Friends** — add friends by username and compare progress head-to-head.
- **Announcements** — this very bell. Release notes now land here automatically on every update.

**Password recovery, without email**

- Reset your password using **security questions** or one-time **recovery codes**.

> Open the account menu (top-right) to reach the leaderboard and friends any time.

Thanks for playing — more flags, more challenges, and more ways to compete are on the way.
