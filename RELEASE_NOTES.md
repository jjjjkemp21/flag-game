## Streamlined progression — chests now reward your flag mastery

*Less clutter, one clear way to grow: learn flags, earn bigger chests.*

**What changed**

- **The XP Road has been retired.** Progression now lives where the learning is — mastery ranks, achievements, and the Atlas Pass — so the home screen is cleaner and there's a single clear path forward.
- **Chest yield now scales with flag mastery.** The more flags you've mastered, the more Atlas Bucks every end-of-run chest pays out — **+5%** once you've mastered 10 flags, climbing to **+25%** when you've mastered the whole world.
- **Your Achievements screen spells it out.** Each mastery milestone now shows the chest-yield bonus it unlocks, and your current bonus sits right at the top of the screen.

*Your XP and Atlas Bucks are untouched. A few beanstalk-themed cosmetics that were exclusive to the XP Road have retired along with it.*

## Polish & fixes — sharper cosmetics, clearer screens

*A big quality pass under the hood and on the surface.*

**Looking sharper**

- **Atlas's cosmetics got a glow-up** — moustaches, beards, lips, and the novelty mouths are redrawn to sit right on Atlas's face, and the Cat-Eye, Aviator, and Rimless glasses plus the Blazing and Electric effects were rebuilt to read crisply.

**Clearer & easier to use**

- **A Home button** now sits in the top bar on every screen, so you're never stranded.
- **Play with the keyboard** in Multiple Choice — press 1–4 or A–D to answer, Enter to move on.
- Clearer focus outlines, bigger tap targets, and better contrast throughout, plus friendlier empty/loading states.
- Removing a friend now asks you to confirm first.

**Fixes**

- Typing a *different* country that's spelled similarly (e.g. Zambia for Gambia) is no longer accepted as correct.
- "Reset Data" no longer wipes the XP and high scores you'd kept.
- Frenzy and Pixelated no longer double-count a record run, and the "New High Score!" banner stays put.
- Tricky flags you'd set aside now resurface so you can actually master them.
- Various scoring, quest, and leaderboard accuracy fixes.

## Globe Mode & geography mastery

*A new way to study — spin the planet, find the country.*

**What's new**

- **Globe Mode** — a 3D world you can spin, zoom, and tap. Pick the country whose flag you're shown, then double-tap (or hit Confirm) to lock it in.
- **Find-on-globe focus** — tap any country and the globe smoothly rotates and semi-zooms onto it, so your guess is unmistakable before you commit.
- **Hint** — stuck? One tap rotates the globe toward the correct continent. Half XP if you still get it right, but your streak stays intact.

**Geography mastery — a whole new axis**

- Globe mode tracks a brand-new "placed on the globe" stat per country, separate from your flag-recognition mastery. Same flags, two skills.
- Your Stats screen now has a Geography Mastery ring next to the existing one.

**New leaderboard scope**

- A **Globe** tab on the leaderboard ranks players by countries placed on the globe — both Global and Friends filters apply.

**New titles & achievements**

- A parallel ladder of geography titles: Pin Dropper → Roamer → Globe Wanderer → Pathfinder → Trailblazer → Topographer → Geomancer → Continental Master → **Atlas Cartographer**. Pick one to wear next to your name.
- 14 new achievements in a fresh "Globe" group, including per-continent Atlases and the legendary Atlas Cartographer for placing every country.
